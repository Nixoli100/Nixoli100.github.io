The Beginning

William Henry gates III was born October 28, 1955 in Seattle. His father was a lawyer and his Mother was a Board of Director for a company called Bancsystem. Early in his life his parents wanted him to get into Law and he regularly attended a church named Congregational Christian Churches. When Gated turned 13 he wrote his first software program.

Microsoft

Gates and Paul Allen started a business together and made a operating system out of BASIC named 86-DOS or just DOS. Microsoft partnered with IBM and shipped many PC's with 86-DOS. On November 20, 1985 Microsoft released windows 1.0
